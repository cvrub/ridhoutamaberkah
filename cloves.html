Product Overview

Prepared for international trade discussions.

Cloves are one of Indonesia's recognized spice commodities and are supplied to international buyers for food, beverage, ingredient, and trading needs.

We discuss grade, dryness, packaging, and documentation according to the buyer's market and destination requirements.

Typical export options

Product formWhole dried cloves
OriginIndonesia
PreparationDrying check, sorting, cleaning, packing
Packaging optionsPP bags, cartons, vacuum or inner liner options by inquiry
Buyer useSpice importer, food processor, trader, ingredient distributor
ShipmentBulk ocean freight or sample shipment by agreement

Specifications are confirmed case by case based on buyer requirements and destination regulations.

Preparation flow

  1. Select dried cloves based on buyer target.
  2. Sort and remove unwanted material as required.
  3. Pack to reduce moisture exposure during transport.
  4. Prepare shipment and documents according to destination requirements.
