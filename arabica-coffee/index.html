Product Overview

Prepared for international trade discussions.

Arabica coffee is offered for international buyers who need flexible specifications, reliable communication, and export-minded preparation.

Available forms include green beans for roasters, roasted beans for distributors, and coffee powder for retail or food service supply. Final specifications can be adjusted based on buyer requirements, quantity, packaging, and destination regulations.

Typical export options

Product formsGreen beans, roasted beans, coffee powder
PreparationSorting, cleaning, grading, roasting or grinding when required
Packaging optionsJute bags, inner liner options, foil bags, cartons, or custom packing by inquiry
Buyer useRoastery, distributor, retail, food service, private-label discussion
ShipmentSamples by air; bulk by ocean freight based on agreement
OriginSumatra, Indonesia

Specifications are confirmed case by case based on buyer requirements and destination regulations.

Preparation flow

  1. Source selected Arabica lots from trusted supply partners.
  2. Sort and prepare beans based on agreed grade and defect tolerance.
  3. For roasted or powder orders, roast profile and grind size are discussed before production.
  4. Pack securely, prepare export documentation, and coordinate shipment to the destination port.
