Product Overview

Prepared for international trade discussions.

Indonesian cinnamon can be supplied in forms discussed with the buyer, including sticks, cuts, or other agreed formats.

We focus on clean preparation, suitable packing, and clear communication from inquiry to shipment.

Typical export options

Product formCinnamon sticks, cut pieces, or buyer-specific form by inquiry
OriginIndonesia
PreparationCleaning, cutting/sorting when required, packing
Packaging optionsCartons, PP bags, inner liner options, customized packing
Buyer useSpice importer, tea and beverage, food ingredient, retail packing
ShipmentBulk ocean freight or sample shipment by agreement

Specifications are confirmed case by case based on buyer requirements and destination regulations.

Preparation flow

  1. Discuss cut size, packaging, and target use.
  2. Prepare and sort cinnamon according to agreed form.
  3. Pack for moisture protection and transport handling.
  4. Coordinate export documentation and freight.
