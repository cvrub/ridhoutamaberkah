Product Overview

Prepared for international trade discussions.

Robusta coffee is suitable for buyers looking for strong body, practical pricing discussions, and flexible order formats.

We support inquiries for green Robusta beans, roasted Robusta beans, and Robusta coffee powder. Each order can be aligned with requested packing, destination requirements, and shipment terms.

Typical export options

Product formsGreen beans, roasted beans, coffee powder
OriginSumatra, Indonesia
PreparationCleaning, sorting, grading, roasting or grinding when required
Packaging optionsExport sacks, liner options, retail packs, cartons, or buyer-specific packaging
Buyer useBlending, instant coffee ingredient programs, retail, food service, wholesale
ShipmentSamples by air; bulk by ocean freight based on agreement

Specifications are confirmed case by case based on buyer requirements and destination regulations.

Preparation flow

  1. Confirm target specification, quantity, and packaging.
  2. Prepare Robusta beans through sorting and quality checks.
  3. Roast or grind according to buyer request when ordering processed coffee.
  4. Pack, label, document, and coordinate international shipment.
